Endless Engines

I created an entry for the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L-v7QPjtyaI">Endless Engines</a> run by Clint Jones.

Given a starter template, using the 3D program of our choice, we were tasked with creating an animation featuring a setting and vehicle of our imagination. I chose to go the scifi route, depicting a scene of a futuristic freeway featuring car chase with a drone as the center piece. The challenge was a valuable way for me to practice modeling, texturing, and animation with a goal in mind.
